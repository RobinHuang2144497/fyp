# fyp
## In recent years, large language models like GPT have made significant progress in the field of natural language processing, particularly with the launch of DeepSeek, which has further demonstrated the immense potential of large models across multiple domains. Pre-trained models have successfully been applied across various disciplines, and as technological iterations accelerate, they are gradually being applied on a wider societal scale. Effective fine-tuning is vital for large models to adapt to different downstream tasks. In the financial sector, previous studies have confirmed that fine-tuning large models has a significant positive impact on financial applications. The primary goal of this study is to fine-tune and deploy the open-source large language model DeepSeek using LLama-Factory in a personal computing environment, in order to build a local large model capable of more accurately analyzing the sentiment of A-share investors. To achieve this, the study created a substantial amount of high-quality fine-tuning datasets and assessed the performance of the fine-tuned model through programmatic evaluations. The results indicate that the fine-tuned model shows a significant improvement in accuracy when assessing investor sentiment, compared to the original model.

## 近年来，GPT等大语言模型在自然语言处理领域取得了显著进展，特别是近年来DeepSeek的问世，进一步展示了大模型在多个领域中的巨大潜力。经过预训练的模型已成功应用于多学科领域，并随着技术迭代加速，逐渐实现社会普及化应用。有效的微调对于大模型适应不同下游任务具有重要意义。在金融领域，先前的研究已证实，微调大模型在金融应用中具有显著的正向效果。本研究的主要目标是在个人计算机环境下，利用LLama-Factory对开源大语言模型DeepSeek进行微调与部署，从而构建一个能更精准判断A股股民情绪信息的本地大模型。为此，本研究创建了大量高质量的微调数据集，并通过程序评估检验了微调后模型的表现。结果表明，经过微调后的模型相比原始模型，在股民情绪信息判断上的精度有了显著提升。
